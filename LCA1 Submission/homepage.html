Famous Temples of India

Sun Temple

Konark, Odisha

Shaped like a colossal chariot with intricately carved wheels and horses, this 13th-century temple is dedicated to the Sun God and showcases extraordinary Odishan architecture.

Learn More

Meenakshi Temple

Madurai, Tamil Nadu

Famous for its towering gopurams (gateway towers) adorned with thousands of vibrant sculptures, this Dravidian-style temple is dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar.

Learn More

Virupaksha Temple

Hampi, Karnataka

Located in Hampi, this temple stands as a majestic reminder of Vijayanagara architecture, with ornate pillars, beautifully sculpted mandapas, and a grand gopuram.

Learn More

Dakshineswar Kali Temple

Kolkata, West Bengal

A stunning example of Bengali Navaratna (Nine-Spired) architecture, this 19th-century temple on the banks of the Hooghly River is dedicated to Goddess Kali.
